Get to Know Us

Zadrugar Berry Fruits is a family-owned business that has been successfully engaged in the purchase and production of berry fruits for many years. Our journey began in the 90s with cultivation and purchase, and we have expanded into freezing, storage, and export of raspberries and blackberries.Our eight-hectare plantation is a testament to our commitment to providing the best berry fruits to our customers.We strive to maintain our position through constant innovation and improvement, ensuring that we always provide our customers with the finest raspberries and blackberries.

Cold Storage

Our capacity is impressive, with 1000 tons of space for storage and 50 tons per day for deep freezing. Our facilities include two storage chambers, two deep freezing tunnels, and two pre-chambers for packaging, all maintained to the highest standards of quality and safety.

Best Quality

We produce and supply only the freshest,highest-quality blackberries and raspberries for maximum flavor and nutrition

Industry Leaders

As industry leaders in both growing and sourcing top-quality blackberries and raspberries, we provide our customers with exceptional fruit

Our Goals

Our goal is to be the trusted source for sustainable and environmentally responsible blackberry and raspberry farming, providing exceptional products and service
