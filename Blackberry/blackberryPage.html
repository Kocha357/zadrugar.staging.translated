Blackberries

❮ ❯

At Zadrugar Berry Fruits, we are proud to offer a premium selection of blackberries sourced from trusted growers. Our selection includes some of the best varieties available, including Triple Crown, Loch Ness, Thornfree and Cacak. To ensure the quality and freshness of our blackberries, we store them in our state-of-the-art cold storage facility until they are ready to be delivered to our customers.

In addition to the blackberries we source from our trusted growers, we also cultivate two varieties on our own 3 hectares of land: Triple Crown and Cacak. Our family takes great care to ensure that these blackberries are grown and harvested with the utmost attention to detail, resulting in a delicious and high-quality product.

If you would like to learn more about our blackberries or place an order, please do not hesitate to contact us. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff are always available to assist you.
