FITOFERT

Read more about this product

Liquid biostimulant fertilizer with an emphasis on phosphorus content. High content of humic, fulvic and amino acids. Enriched with algae extract. It contains microelements. Ideal for combining with starter fertilizers in fertigation. The main purpose for foliar treatments at the beginning of the growing season, but also after harvest for better preparation of plant fertility in the following year.

GLIFOMARK

Read more about this product

A non-selective herbicide, it is taken up by the leaf mass. It moves up and down in the plant, it is inactivated in contact with the soil. It inhibits EPSP, i.e. the synthesis of aromatic acids, thus preventing the synthesis of amino acids necessary for protein production.

LUNA SENSATION

Read more about this product

The specific mode of action comes as a result of the combination of two different fungicidal substances. The substance Fluopiram belongs to succinate dehydrogenase enzyme inhibitors (SDHI), thus blocking breathing, i.e. energy production in the cells of the fungi it acts on. The active substance Trifloxystrobin, belonging to the group of strobilurins, interferes with electron transport in the mitochondria of pathogen cells and adversely affects the respiration process of the phytopathogenic fungus.

SATURN TERRA

Read more about this product

Saturn Terra is a non-systemic insecticide for controlling soil insects. The active substance chlorpyrifos belongs to the group of organophosphates, which act as inhibitors of the activity of acetylcholinesterase of the central nervous system of insects. This mechanism enables a wide range of actions. Physico-chemical properties of chlorpyrifos provide contact, digestive and respiratory action. Chlorpyrifos in Saturn Terra was applied to corn grits. Corn grits as a carrier of organic origin has the property of an attractant, to attract harmful insects, but also to enable an easier and even introduction of the preparation into the soil.

TEPPEKI

Read more about this product

Insecticide-aphicid from the Pyridinecarboxamide group with contact and digestive action. The unique mode of action prevents the creation of cross-resistance with insecticides for the same purposes. In the treated plant, it moves acropetally and translaminarly. It has a contact and gastric effect after 1 hour of ingestion, and death occurs after 2 to 5 days, depending on the pest's starvation.
