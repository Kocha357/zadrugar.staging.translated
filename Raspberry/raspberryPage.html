From our trusted growers in Serbia, we have carefully selected the best varieties of raspberries available, including Willamette, Tulameen, Fertodi, Polka, Himbo Top, Glen, and Meeker. These raspberries are all kept in our state-of-the-art cold storage facility to ensure that they retain their freshness and quality until they reach our customers.

In addition to offering raspberries from our trusted growers, we also cultivate two varieties on our own 5 hectares of land: Willamete and Fertodi. These varieties are carefully grown and harvested by our family, ensuring that they meet our exacting standards for taste and quality.

If you need more infromation about our Raspberries, or if you would like to place an order, we would be happy to hear from you.
